From Coffee to Covers

One-liner:
A vibrant virtual fellowship turning simple conversations into lasting Rotaract connections.

Small description:
From Coffee to Covers was a joint virtual fellowship hosted by the Rotaract Club of Rajalakshmi Engineering College in collaboration with the Rotaract Club of MOP Vaishnav College for Women. The event focused on strengthening inter-club bonding through interactive icebreakers, fun challenges, and open conversations. Activities like “A Day in the Life of a Rotaractor,” Spin the Wheel, and emoji-based camera challenges sparked laughter, engagement, and genuine connection. The session extended beyond its scheduled time, reflecting the energy and enthusiasm of the participants, and concluded with warm reflections symbolizing camaraderie and collaboration.

Date: 21st October 2025
Venue: Google Meet (Online)
